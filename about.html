A life shaped by grace, selfless medical service, and lived remembrance of Neem Karoli Maharaj.

Still serving, in new forms

Today, the service continues through satsang, documentation, and projects that support people and dharma.

Shraddha (Reverence)

A life shaped by Neem Karoli Maharaj’s grace, lived through remembrance, satsang, and sharing prasad of experience.

Dharma (Stewardship)

Active involvement in dharmic and social causes, including temple initiatives and service on trust boards, turning faith into responsibility.

Divine Nectar

Talks, satsangs, and stories shared with sincerity.
